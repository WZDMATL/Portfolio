Mission

Constitute Freedom is a civic association within the meaning of Section 501(c)(4) whose mission is to end caste politics by amending and revising global, national, and state constitutions to reflect unbarred and equal-for-all political and economic systems.

A civic association is a group of citizens who have voluntarily joined together for collective action on community affairs. In general, its objectives are to preserve, enhance, and plan for the orderly development of the neighborhood and to promote the general welfare, safety and civil spirit of the community.

Vision

To see nations practice opportune-for-all independence and freedom for all groups, eradicating denationalization, hate and war.

Purpose

Constitute Freedom is a political advocacy group with a mission to end caste politics by shedding light on its detrimental effects on society and amending and revising global, national, and state constitutions and laws to reflect unbarred and equal-for-all political and economic systems worldwide.

Purpose
  • 1. To ensure laws are passed that end discrimination
  • 2. To nationalize “black” and “colored” peoples in America by federal, state and local recognition of heritage
  • 3. To rally around uniting citizens and undesirables on a foundation of love and freedom for all.
  • 4. To eradicate nation-on-nation hate and war through love and unity advocacy.
